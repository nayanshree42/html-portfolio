The Best Anime Movies According to Nayanshree

My favourite anime movies of all-time.

Your Name

This movie simply a masterpiece.

Grave of the Fireflies

This movie was possibly the best war movie i have ever seen.

Wolf Children

This movie truly speaks volums from presenting a totally different virtue of single motherhood to the concern of unexpected ignorance of our nature.

Violet Evergarden

It's truly a masterpiece. The strigest women ever seen on screens.

A Silent Voice

Beautiful and heartwarming story with characters that felt real.

Weathering with You

This movie portrays a different side of adolescence where one questions where they want to be, who they want to be, and who will be beside them as they chase what form of hapiness they can find.

Josee, the Tiger and the Fish

Josee was a very calming movie about a tender loving relationship between a wheelchair bound woman and a poor college student.

I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

The film hits all the right notes emotionally.

Whisper of the Heart

Whisper of the Heart is a coming of the age love story at its core and a really good movie.

5 Centimetres per Second

This movie really speaks to the soul about people and the inevitability of life.
